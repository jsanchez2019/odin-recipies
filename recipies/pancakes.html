Pancakes

Description

These easy pancakes don't require much thought early in the morning and taste great!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
